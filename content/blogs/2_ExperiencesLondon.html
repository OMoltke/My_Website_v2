Making friendships while making a difference

On Friday the 3rd of September, Stream B of the MFA 2022 went to help the Splash Projects rebuild an old playground as well as tidy up some gardening areas.

This was a great opportunity to meet our new fellow students while making a positive impact and get some physical work in. My group, specifically, was group B4.

The work was carried out in Swedenborg Gardens, London, which you can see on the map below.

Group B4

It was the first time, I was going to meet my study group, group B4.

The group consists of Daria from Russia, Leilah from the UK, Yuna from China, Martin from Bulgaria, Allen from China, Neel from India, and myself, Otto, from Denmark. So we are a very diverse group.
