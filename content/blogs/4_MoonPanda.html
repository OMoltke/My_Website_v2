Moon Panda

My brother, Gustav, and his wife, Maddy, have a band together. The band is called Moon Panda.

Maddy is from San Diego, California and Gustav is from Haslev, Denmark. They met at Berklee College of Music in Boston, USA, where they both studied their undergraduate. Now, they are married, live in Denmark, and have a dog named BMO.

They have just released their newest song Vacationer on Spotify along with its music video on Youtube.

Vacationer is the first single for their upcoming album What on Earth, which can be preordered on their webshop. Here, you can also buy other essentials, such as t-shirts, socks, and tea. However, if you want to get a bang for your buck, you should just go for the Ultimate Fanda Bundle.

Designed for the super-smart consumer. Wow, you’re smart if you go for this one! -Moon Panda on their Ultimate Fanda Bundle
